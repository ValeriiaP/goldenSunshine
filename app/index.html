About Golden sunshine resort

Welcome to the Golden Sunshine hotel inVancouver. Our staff is always here to help; no request is too big or too small. We make it our mission to provide you with an attentive service tailored to meet your needs.

Golden Sunshine hotel is a newly renovated boutique-style hotel situated in the heart of Vancouver's entertainment district. This Vancouver, Canada hotel is minutes from BC Place Stadium, Granville Island, the Vancouver Convention & Exhibition Centre, the University of British Columbia, Grouse Mountain, Stanley Park and Vancouver International Airport.

There is a diverse selection of restaurants and lounges surrounding the hotel within this enlivened neighborhood. As an added benefit, our guests receive free VIP access to the on-site The Cellar nightclub and The Roxy cabaret. This includes an advance line and no cover before 10 p.m. Guests also receive a 25 percent discount at the on-site restaurant, Doolin's Irish Pub, before 6 p.m.

This Vancouver, Canada hotel provides amenities and features like:

  • Discounted day passes to Steve Nash Fitness World & Sports Club
  • Free wired and wireless high-speed Internet access

Nestling in what can be only described as a Garden of Eden, Golden Sunset Hotel offers discerning couples and families a home away from home to relax and unwind.

The hotel is one of the few in the area to offer a full size swimming pool and children's pool. All modern conveniences are provided from hairdryers, TV, to free wireless internet access.

This 100 percent smoke-free and eco-friendly heritage building blends the contemporary designs and warmth among vibrant colors and décor. All rooms include free safes, pillow-top mattresses, flat-screen televisions with high-definition cable programming, motion sensor climate control and air conditioning, work desks, wake-up service, coffee makers, irons, ironing boards and hair dryers. Some rooms include refrigerators, fireplaces and hot tubs. Breakfast available at a nominal fee and it may vary.

No matter what the nature of your visit may be or if you are traveling with with family, friends or on business, Golden Sunset hotel is the perfect location for your stay in Vancouver, BC.

Facilities and Services

Spa Utopia

Refresh your senses with customized services in a sanctuary reminiscent of Ancient Rome at Spa Utopia. Featuring towering pillars and cascading waterfalls, this beautifully appointed urban oasis is the big spa.

Fitness Club

Enjoy invigorating workouts at our fitness centre with state-of-the-art equipment and free weights or explore the city by following maps of suggested jogging routes provided by our Team.

Swimming Pool

Savour spectacular views overlooking the harbour, mountains and Stanley Park as you swim in our heated outdoor salt water pool, soak in the hot tub or lounge on the sundeck. Indoor saunas are also available for you to relax in and enjoy the good life.
